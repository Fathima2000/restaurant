About us

Information about us

THE YEAR – 2018
THE WOMEN – Alexa
THE PLACE – A small restaurant housed in BTM 2ND STAGE, Bangalore.

This is where we began…

Back in 2018, Alexa, armed with a strong background in the catering business, came to India from Malaysia. She opened the first Food in my tummy Restaurant in BTM 2ND STAGE, Bangalore, which humbly served healthy & authentic recipes, alongside the newspaper and early morning banter. Her eager customers just came by for a hot, meal, or even Chicken and Mutton! Here was a women with not only a passion for food but also a keen business sense.
